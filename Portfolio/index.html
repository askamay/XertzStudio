Xertz Game Studio is a mobile game development company. It specializes in developing games based on genres such as the card, sports adventure, and action. Some of the games developed by the company are Teen Patti Multiplayer, Hearts Ace Bhabhi Thulla, Lets Play Mini Golf, and King of Fatal Combat. It develops games for Android, iOS and Windows platforms. It generates revenue through advertisements and in-app purchases.
